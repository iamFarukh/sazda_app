Surah Al-Kahf
VERSE 1

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَنزَلَ عَلَىٰ عَبْدِهِ الْكِتَابَ وَلَمْ يَجْعَل لَّهُ عِوَجًا ۜ

"[All] praise is [due] to Allah, who has sent down upon His Servant the Book and has not made therein any deviance."
